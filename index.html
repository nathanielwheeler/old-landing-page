About

Before I was a developer, I was an art student, a geology major, a calculus tutor, a bookkeeper, and even a coroner’s assistant.

I am a knowledge sponge that isn’t content unless I am learning or puzzling over something.

When I’m not working on projects or looking at documentation, I like to make comics, read some good ol’ scifi/fantasy, and partake in outdoor activities.

I live in Boise, Idaho with my wife and our two cats.
